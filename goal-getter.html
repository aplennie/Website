Goal-Getter

May 2020

The second project of my Coursera specialization, this time for the completion of the "UX Design Fundamentals" course.

We were tasked with creating a simple app that solved a problem in our lives. I chose to make a productivity app to track goal progress.

After going through an onslaught of productivity apps on the app store, I found a few that I was inspired by, especially by one called "Habitica".
