Puck Cakes

June 2020

Puck Cakes is a restaurant website concept I created for the final two courses in the Coursera UI/UX Specialization.

The only criteria we were given was to create a full set of mockups for a fake restaurant, particularily with a customizable order system. I wanted to do cupcakes (with a twist) because they're fun and super customizable.

The main demographic was a youthful / Gen-Z type group, so I wanted to focus on social media and "Instagrammable" food. Users can create their own cute little "Puck Cake" with an interactive online order process and get it delivered to them within the hour.

My favorite part of this concept and brand was the whole idea that they're not cupcakes at all... even down to the domain (notcupcakes.com).
